About GlobalPolicy Institute

Driving Evidence-Based Policy

GlobalPolicy Institute is a leading independent research organization dedicated to advancing knowledge on critical policy challenges facing the world. Since 2008, we have been at the forefront of rigorous research, strategic analysis, and policy engagement.

Our multidisciplinary team of researchers, economists, and policy experts works across multiple sectors to produce high-quality research that informs decision-makers worldwide.

We believe in the power of evidence-based policy and are committed to making our research accessible and actionable for policymakers, practitioners, and the public.
