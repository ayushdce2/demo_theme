Who We Are?

Lightworks Education Pvt. Ltd. is a modern and progressive Ed Startup with its registered office in Pune, Maharashtra, India. We are stubborn at the core in our vision of initiating human evolutionary programs of higher learning and wisdom, and in making it an integral part of the educational discourse in Indian classrooms. We work on the need to prioritize daily Mindfulness and Meditation practices starting from the seed level. We believe that every student deserves the tools to thrive both academically and personally. It has inspired us to develop an evidence – based curriculum that caters to all K-12 grades, aligning seamlessly with the NEP 2020 and NCF 2023.


What We Do?

At Lightworks Education Pvt. Ltd., we specialize in crafting innovative curriculum and training programs that blend ancient mindfulness and meditation wisdom with cutting-edge scientific research. Our mission is to foster mental well-being and integrate modern spiritual and scientific knowledge into everyday life.


Mindfulness is the practice of maintaining conscious awareness of your thoughts, emotions, and bodily sensations in the present moment, free from judgment. In essence, it’s about focusing all your attention on the here and now.


Recent scientific research suggests that mindfulness is deeply embedded in our DNA, revealing our inherent ability to live in the present and fully appreciate life. It’s considered the most powerful human capability, crucial for navigating transformative times.


Meditation involves concentrating your attention on a single point for an extended period, training your mind to recognize and release unhelpful thoughts. While meditation is a daily practice, mindfulness is a skill to be employed throughout the day.


Lightworks is at the forefront of integrating these practices into schools across India, leading with authenticity, integrity, and harmony to revolutionize education and well-being.

How We Do It?

At Lightworks Education Pvt. Ltd., we are dedicated to making mindfulness accessible and impactful through a range of tailored programs. Our approach ensures that individuals from all walks of life can experience the transformative benefits of mindfulness. Here’s how we bring our vision to life:


1. Introductory Program

Our Introductory Course is designed to provide an insightful introduction to mindfulness for schools. This short-term program offers educators and students a foundational understanding of mindfulness practices and their benefits. It serves as a preliminary step to demonstrate the effectiveness of mindfulness and prepare schools for more extensive integration.

2. Training the Teachers

We offer specialized training programs for educators, equipping them with the skills to effectively teach the course material pertaining to their classes. Our "Training the Teachers" program spans 30 hours and includes interactive workshops, practical exercises, and ongoing support. This training empowers teachers to create a mindful learning environment and enhance their own well-being, which in turn benefits their students.

3. Mindfulness for Students - K to 12

Our comprehensive Mindfulness for Students program is designed for all grade levels, from kindergarten through 12th grade. This curriculum includes age-appropriate mindfulness practices and techniques that support emotional regulation, focus, and resilience. The program is delivered through engaging classroom sessions and supports students in developing essential life skills for academic and personal success.

4. Mindfulness for All - DIY E-Learning Portal

For individuals seeking flexibility, our DIY E-Learning Portal provides an accessible and self-paced mindfulness course. This online platform offers a variety of resources, including recorded sessions and interactive materials, designed to fit into any schedule. Whether you’re a student, professional, or simply interested in personal growth, our e-learning portal makes mindfulness training available to everyone, regardless of location or background.
