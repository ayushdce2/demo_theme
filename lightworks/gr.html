Gloria Raut

IMMA Certified Creative Mindfulness Teacher | Educator | Soft Skill Trainer

Gloria Raut is a distinguished Faculty Leader with a robust career spanning 29 years in education, specializing in computer applications and science. Her extensive experience also includes 3 years as a Soft Skill Trainer and 4 years as a Quality Coach, highlighting her versatility and commitment to professional development.


Certified as a Creative Mindfulness Teacher by IMMA, Gloria integrates mindfulness practices into her educational approach, enhancing both personal and academic growth for students and professionals alike. Her expertise spans from guiding K-12 students to mentoring skilled professionals, reflecting her comprehensive ability to adapt and excel across diverse learning environments.


Gloria’s multifaceted experience and dedication to holistic education position her as a key leader in shaping and advancing faculty initiatives, fostering a well-rounded and impactful educational experience
