Mindfulness Introductory Program

Our introductory mindfulness program is designed to provide a comprehensive yet impactful introduction to mindfulness for both educators and students. This foundational program offers a robust overview of mindfulness practices, aiming to foster a deep understanding of its principles and benefits. It consists of 10 hours of classroom sessions tailored for teachers and 16 hours dedicated to students. These sessions are crafted to lay a solid groundwork, preparing educators and students for a more in-depth exploration of mindfulness in their ongoing education.

For Educators:

Our 10-hour teacher training sessions provide a thorough introduction to mindfulness practices and their application within the classroom. Key components of this training include:


Fundamentals of Mindfulness

Understanding the core principles of mindfulness and its relevance in education.


Teaching Techniques

Practical strategies for integrating mindfulness into daily classroom routines.


Value System Integration

How to infuse mindfulness values into teaching practices and school culture.


Link to Ancient Wisdom

Exploring the connection between mindfulness practices and ancient meditation traditions.


Classroom Management

Techniques to create a mindful learning environment that supports student well-being and focus.


Practical Exercises

Hands-on activities and mindfulness exercises to implement immediately in the classroom.

For Students:

Our 16-hour student sessions are designed to introduce mindfulness in an engaging and age-appropriate manner. Students will learn:


Basics of Mindfulness

Simple techniques to enhance self-awareness and manage stress.


Mindfulness Activities

Interactive exercises to practice mindfulness and develop focus.


Emotional Regulation

Strategies to handle emotions and build resilience.


Connection to Learning

How mindfulness can improve concentration, memory, and academic performance.


Personal Growth

Encouragement to apply mindfulness in daily life for personal development and well-being.


This introductory program is the first step in a journey towards integrating mindfulness deeply into one’s personal and educational experience. By participating in these sessions, both educators and students will gain a foundational understanding of mindfulness, setting the stage for a richer, more sustained engagement with mindfulness practices in the classroom.

The Process

  • 1. Book a Free Demo
  • 2. Signup for the Introductory Mindfulness Program
  • 3. Nominate the Educators for the program
  • 4. Select any grade/s from K to 12th for the student program
  • 5. Allocate classrooms
  • 6. Finalize a timetable for both sessions
  • 7. Sessions delivery
  • 8. Become a MKP
  • 9. Introduce the curriculum across grades in your school
  • 10. Get your educators and students certified year-on-year

    • As of Aug 2024, the Lightworks Education curriculum is the first and only academic textbook-based program in Mindfulness for schools in India. Designed with the unique sensibilities and existing syllabus of students in the sub-continent in mind, this program also addresses the practical challenges faced by today’s educators. The Lightworks Mindfulness Program seamlessly integrates with the current educational framework, enhancing students' lives with a higher sense of mindfulness and well-being. We are currently securing affiliations and accreditations from the Education Ministry of India and from various other educational bodies in India and beyond.
