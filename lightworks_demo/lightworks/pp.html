Privacy Policy for Lightworks Mindfulness India Private Limited

Effective Date: 1st Sep 2024


Welcome to Lightworks Mindfulness India Private Limited ("we," "our," "us"). We are committed to safeguarding your privacy on this website. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with us and/or our services, including our mindfulness curriculum for schools, teachers, and students via textbooks, as well as our eLearning offerings for individuals, corporates, and various kinds of institutes.

Welcome to the Lightworks Mindfulness family.


User Provided Information

When you register for our Application, Website, or Services, you provide us with certain personal information, including your name, age, email address, location, phone number, password, and your ward’s educational interests. Additionally, we collect transaction-related information, communication details, and data you input during use, such as when participating in discussions or taking tests. This information is categorized as “Personal Information,” “Sensitive Personal Information,” and “Associated Information” as defined under the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011 (the “Data Protection Rules”). Collectively, these are referred to as ‘Information’ in this Policy.

We use your Information to provide Services, communicate important updates, and send marketing promotions. We do not differentiate between users based on device access, so ensuring you log in with your credentials is essential for accurate Information capture. You may choose not to provide Personal or Sensitive Personal Information, and you may withdraw consent for its use at any time by contacting us. However, withdrawal of consent may affect your ability to use the Application or Services.


1. Information We Collect

Our Privacy Policy encompasses the information you provide us, including but not limited to:

● Personal Information: Includes your name, email address, physical address, phone number, and any other personal details you provide when you register, purchase product or service, enroll in a course, browse the site or contact us.

● Transactional Information: Includes payment details and billing information related to textbook purchases and eLearning course fees. This also includes content from messages and comments, as well as transactional data such as credit/debit card, UPI and other payment wallets, and billing information.

● Usage Data: Includes information about your interactions with our website, such as data related to your site activity, device used, browser type, location, IP address, pages visited, cookies, browser type, pages visited, time spent on our site, and other similar data.

● Communication Data: Includes the content of your communications with us, including emails, feedback, and other correspondence.


2. How We Use Your Information

● To Provide and Manage Our Services: Including processing orders, delivering textbooks, and providing access to eLearning sessions.

● To Improve Our Offerings: By analyzing usage data and feedback to enhance our website, platforms, approach, methodology, curriculum and eLearning content.

● To Communicate with You: Including sending updates, newsletters, and promotional materials related to our services. This includes communications to the referrals you provide.

● To Process Transactions: Ensuring secure and efficient handling of payments.

● ●To Support and Respond to Inquiries: Addressing your questions and providing customer support.


3. How We Protect Your Information

● Data Usage: We utilize your information to manage our services, communicate with you, and for advertising and marketing purposes. We may use your information to send email newsletters, provide marketing and promotional content, personalize your experience with relevant content, and improve our website and services. This includes responding to customer service requests, managing contests and promotions, processing transactions, communicating about orders or services, and following up on inquiries.

● Data Sharing: We may share non-personally identifiable data, such as aggregated user statistics, with third parties.

● Data Retention: We retain information only as long as necessary for business operations and legal compliance.

● Data Encryption: We use HTTPS to encrypt data transmitted between your browser and our website.

● Secure Storage: Your personal and payment information is stored in secure, access-controlled environments.

● Regular Audits: We regularly review our security practices and update our systems to protect against vulnerabilities.
