Course Overview

Our TTT program is a 30-hour plug-and-play module delivered through interactive E-learning sessions. This course is meticulously designed to provide educators with a deep understanding of mindfulness principles and practical strategies that are explained in the course material of their classes.


The Process

Initial Assessment

  • We start with a detailed discussion of the current challenges faced by educators in imparting knowledge to their pupils. We then present them with an overview of how Mindfulness is a proven way to help them overcome everyday challenges and disburse their duties to their true potential.

Detailed Curriculum Overview & Teaching Methodology

  • Our TTT curriculum is tailored to address specific requirements for teachers in order to impart mindfulness education to students effortlessly and organically. Each batch of teachers will be trained specifically on how to teach the books pertaining to their classes/grades.

Interactive Workshops

  • Teachers engage in a series of dynamic exercises based on key topics from their mindfulness textbooks. These activities provide teachers with a clear understanding of how to effectively teach these concepts to their students.

Practical Application

  • Educators learn how to incorporate mindfulness practices into their daily routines and classroom activities, fostering a supportive and focused learning environment.

Certification

  • Post-training, each teacher receives a course completion certification along with other mementos and recognitions earned during the course time.

c

  • Lightworks is also working on another program to offer continuous support and resources to teachers, even after the initial course. This will upgrade their knowledge from time-to-time and help them integrate more mindfulness topics seamlessly into their teaching methodology.

Delivery

The training is delivered through a series of dynamic E-learning sessions. These interactive sessions are designed to be engaging and practical, providing teachers with hands-on experience in applying creative mindfulness techniques in their classrooms. The delivery format ensures that educators gain both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, ready to implement immediately with their students.

Benefits

To Teachers:

  • Enhanced Well-Being : Reduces stress and enhances emotional resilience, helping teachers manage the demands of their profession more effectively.
  • Improved Focus :Techniques to increase concentration and presence, leading to more effective teaching and classroom management.
  • Professional Growth : Develops new skills and strategies that enhance personal and professional development.

To Students:

  • Increased Emotional Regulation : Helps students manage their emotions better, leading to improved behavior and academic performance.
  • Enhanced Concentration :Mindfulness practices foster a more focused and engaged classroom environment.
  • Development of Self-awareness : Students learn to be aware of themselves and their surroundings, helping them to stay holistically connected in the present moment.
  • Greater Resilience :Students develop coping skills and resilience, equipping them to handle academic and personal challenges more effectively.

To the School:

  • Positive Learning Environment : Integrates mindfulness to create a more supportive and harmonious school atmosphere thereby facilitating a profound positive transformation all around.
  • Holistic Education :Enriches the overall educational experience by addressing the emotional and psychological needs of both teachers and students.
  • Increased Engagement : Promotes a proactive approach to student well-being, contributing to a more engaged and motivated school community.
